Troubleshooting (External prerequisites only)

Required free prerequisites:
- F4SE
- KoboldCPP
- A loaded GGUF model

- KoboldCPP not running on localhost:5001:
  Start KoboldCPP and load a GGUF model.
- F4SE not loaded:
  Launch Fallout 4 through f4se_loader.exe.
- Bridge did not start:
  Run Data/F4AI/Launch_F4AI_Bridge.bat and check for missing executable warnings.
